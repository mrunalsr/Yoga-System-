PLEASE FOLLOW THESE STEPS

1. Lie down on a flat surface.

2. Shimmy your hips as close as possible to the wall, then start walking your feet up the wall until your body forms an L-shape.

3. Make adjustments so that the posture feels relaxing. Use a pillow under your head or a towel at the base of your back.

4. Stay in this position for 5-6 minutes.

5. Perform this daily for 20 minutes.
